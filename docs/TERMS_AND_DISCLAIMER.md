# Terms And Disclaimer

Last updated: June 13, 2026

RiskWise is an educational tool for reviewing options risk before making a trade decision. It is not a brokerage, investment adviser, or trade execution platform.

## Not Financial Advice

RiskWise does not tell users to buy, sell, hold, enter, or exit any security or options contract. Any scores, explanations, agent summaries, or AI responses are educational decision-support outputs and should not be treated as financial advice.

## Market Data Limits

RiskWise may use delayed, estimated, reference-only, or user-provided market data. Live OPRA options data, provider-reported Greeks, bid/ask, volume, open interest, and implied volatility may be missing unless a supported provider is configured. Missing data should be shown to the user instead of hidden.

## User Responsibility

Users are responsible for verifying all market data, contract details, risk calculations, and suitability before making any financial decision. Options trading involves substantial risk and can result in loss of the full premium or more depending on strategy.

## AI Limitations

RiskWiseAI can explain concepts, summarize risk factors, and ask follow-up questions. It may be wrong, incomplete, delayed, or dependent on missing data. It should be used as a learning and review aid, not as an authority.

## Beta Status

The current app is a beta/mockup-stage product. Features, data sources, calculations, and storage behavior may change before public release.
